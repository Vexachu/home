Lesson 1: What is Scratch?

Tour the editor and make your first animation.

Step 1

Open scratch.mit.edu and click 'Create'. Explore: Stage (top-right), Sprites list (bottom-right), and Blocks (left).

1 / 3

Step 2

Add a new sprite or use the Cat. Use Events → 'when green flag clicked' and Motion/Looks blocks to move and say hello.

2 / 3

Step 3

Make it interactive: add 'when space key pressed' to jump or play a sound. Save your project and give it a name!
